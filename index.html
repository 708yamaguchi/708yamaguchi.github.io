Biography

Naoya Yamaguchi is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechano-Informatics of Graduate School of Information Science and Technology at The University of Tokyo. He received B.E. in Mechanical Engineering at The University of Tokyo in 2018. He received M.S. in Mechano-Informatics at The University of Tokyo in 2020. He receives a JSPS Research Fellowship for Young Scientists DC1. His research interests include sensing mechanism and manipulation strategy for daily assistive robots.
